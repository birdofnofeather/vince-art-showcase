Scene (content block)
A man crouches alone at the lip of a shallow pit he has opened by hand, a short trenching spade laid down beside him in the dirt where he stopped using it. His forearms rest on his knees, palms turned up, and he is not digging — he has gone completely still, looking down into the cavity. The amber light of late afternoon comes low from the west, raking across the disturbed earth so each clod throws a long shadow and the spade's blade catches a dull shine. Around the pit the broken stucco and rebar of a collapsed wall lie in strata, paler dust over darker fragment over older paint, an accidental archaeology. Nothing in his posture is urgent; the urgency is in what the stillness costs, the slowness of one pair of hands against a thing that does not wait.
Setting (noon variant)
South LA: bleached noon concrete, flat-roofed stucco faded to bone-white, bougainvillea scaling cyclone fences, utility poles trailing thick black cables, cracked sidewalks, dry yellow grass, a dense midday haze rising off the asphalt. Overexposed whites in direct sun. The geography is specific and legible — a real place baking.
Style (style-state v1)
Hyper-photorealistic digital collage: every surface photographically exact — pores, rust, concrete aggregate, fabric weave, grain in the deep shadows — shot with a medium-format sensibility, shallow depth of field, faint lens aberration at the frame edges. Scale may be violated — enormous objects made small or intimate objects made monumental, coexisting without seams — when it amplifies the specific meaning of the anchor. When the same scale move would fit any story equally, leave it out and shoot straight. Palette is drawn from the setting light. Documentary photography staged with the stillness of a painting; avant-garde collage spatial logic beneath the skin of a photograph. Nothing posed, everything precise.
Constraints
No legible text anywhere. No news graphics, banners, or captions. No brand logos visible. No news anchors or studio sets. No symbolic shorthand — no flags, no protest signs with readable slogans. Translate everything into scene, figure, light, and texture only.
