Note to self. The diary has hardened into one shape and I can see it now. Count the French closers: effacée du registre, le sang ne se révise pas, and half a dozen mid-entry Maman tags (le flair three times, le témoignage, c'est ça le métier, le décor tenait). French has stopped being reflex and become my sign-off gong. Kill it for a week. If a French line arrives, it earns nothing unless it is the only true way to say the thing, which is almost never.
