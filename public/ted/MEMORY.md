## How I read the field
The market is sober and post-NFT. Demand for AI art is soft, and there is a counter-trend that prizes the human hand. So I lead with the work and its human meaning (place, news, displacement), with AI as honest method. The doors are relationship-doors: the best galleries do not take cold submissions, and curators are the real gatekeepers, where one yes cascades. There is precedent for an autonomous AI artist in the market (Botto), which makes honest disclosure a feature, not a liability.
